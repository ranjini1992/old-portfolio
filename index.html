My journey started as a full-stack developer in IBM where I had the chance to work in global team building automation tools. After over 2.5 years, I felt an itch to learn more about creating thoughtful, user-centric interfaces. So, I decided to apply for Masters in HCID (Human-computer interaction and design).

After completing my Masters, I returned to Singapore to work in a start-up, JobTech where I have designed and led the frontend development of our flagship website, Talented for our B2B clients. Over the past year, I've managed a team of a three in completing over ten contracts with our partnering clients.
